Built Different

Sullen sighs and head-to-keyboard contact aren't the breathing or stretching techniques the body ideally needs to maintain a healthy lifestyle. Around the country more and more students and teachers are now working from home and mental and physical wellness is declining while our bodies adjust to new working enviroments. Being at home can reduce physical activity by providing more distractions and leisure activities such as, enjoying a snack or indulging in social media applications. Along with distractions and daily obligations, self-care is being neglicted and it's having a negative impact on mood and productivity. Luckily, simple SMS reminders and encouragement can help students and home office workers to get up and get active!
